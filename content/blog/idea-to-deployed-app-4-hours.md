# From Idea to Deployed App in 4 Hours

Sometimes the best way to validate an idea is to build it. That's exactly what we did with Habit Tracker—a personal health tracking app built and deployed in a single afternoon.

## The Challenge

Track daily health metrics:
- Weight
- Walking distance
- Water intake
- Mood

Keep it simple. Make it work. Ship it.

## The Stack

- **Laravel** — Rapid backend development
- **Vue.js + Inertia.js** — SPA feel without API complexity
- **Tailwind CSS** — Fast styling
- **SQLite** — Zero-config database for personal use
- **Chart.js** — Progress visualization

## What Made It Possible

### 1. Clear Scope

We defined exactly what we needed. Nothing more.

### 2. Proven Patterns

No experiments. Use what we know works.

### 3. AI Assistance

AI handled boilerplate while we focused on the unique parts.

## Lessons Learned

1. **Constraints drive creativity** — Limited time forced smart decisions
2. **Ship early** — Real usage reveals what matters
3. **Iterate later** — Perfect is the enemy of shipped

The Habit Tracker is now a daily tool. Not bad for four hours' work.
