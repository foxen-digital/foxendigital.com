# Building AI-Powered Development Workflows

AI assistants have transformed how we build software. Not by replacing developers, but by amplifying what they can do. Here's how Foxen Digital integrates AI into our workflow.

## What is Agentic Engineering?

At Foxen Digital, we define agentic engineering as:

> A software development discipline where developers define high-level goals and constraints, and AI agents autonomously handle entire workflows under human supervision.

It's delegation, not just assistance. The human remains in the loop for approval, direction, and quality control.

## Our Workflow

### 1. Specification

We write clear, detailed specifications. AI needs context to be useful.

### 2. Implementation

AI handles boilerplate, tests, and initial implementation. We review and refine.

### 3. Review

Every line gets human eyes. AI is fast, but humans catch what machines miss.

## The Results

- **Faster delivery** — Routine tasks take hours, not days
- **Higher quality** — More time for review means better code
- **Better documentation** — AI excels at explaining what code does

## What AI Can't Do

- Understand business context without guidance
- Make architectural trade-offs
- Navigate office politics
- Replace human judgment

The future isn't AI vs developers. It's developers with AI vs developers without.
