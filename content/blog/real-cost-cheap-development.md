# The Real Cost of Cheap Development

Everyone wants a bargain. But when it comes to software development, cheap upfront often means expensive later. Here's what we've learned from rescuing projects that went wrong.

## The Hidden Costs

### Technical Debt

Quick fixes accumulate. Before long, simple changes take weeks. The codebase becomes fragile, and developers fear touching anything.

### Knowledge Loss

Budget developers move on. Without documentation or clean architecture, the next team starts from scratch.

### Opportunity Cost

While you're fixing broken code, competitors are shipping features.

## The Quality Equation

```
Total Cost = Development + Maintenance + Opportunity Cost
```

Cheap development minimizes the first term but maximizes the others.

## Our Approach

We're not the cheapest option. But we aim to be the best value:

- Clean, documented code
- Test coverage that gives confidence
- Architecture that scales
- Knowledge transfer built in

The result? Software that grows with your business, not against it.
